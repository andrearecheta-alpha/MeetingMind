# MeetingMind — Guest Viewer Setup Guide

Welcome! Your host is using MeetingMind to run a live meeting and has invited you to view real-time fact-checks and key facts as the conversation happens.

To connect remotely, you need to install a free tool called **Tailscale** (takes about 3 minutes).

---

## What is Tailscale?

Tailscale is a free, secure networking app that lets your device connect directly to your host's machine — without exposing anything to the public internet.

---

## Step 1 — Install Tailscale

1. Go to **[tailscale.com/download](https://tailscale.com/download)**
2. Download the version for your operating system (Windows, Mac, iOS, Android)
3. Install and open the app
4. Sign in with a Google or Microsoft account (free, no subscription needed)

---

## Step 2 — Ask Your Host for the Guest Link

Your host will send you a link that looks like this:

```
http://100.x.x.x:8000/guest/view/xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx
```

Make sure you have received this link before the meeting starts.

---

## Step 3 — Connect to the Meeting

1. Confirm Tailscale shows **"Connected"** in your system tray or menu bar
2. Open the guest link in any browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox)
3. Enter your name when prompted
4. Click **"Join Meeting"**

You will now see:
- **Fact Check panel** — live cards flagging any claims that differ from the host's knowledge base
- **Key Facts panel** — important numbers, names, and decisions captured in real time

---

## What You Can and Cannot Do

| ✅ You can | ❌ You cannot |
|-----------|--------------|
| View fact-check cards live | See the transcript |
| View key facts as they appear | Access coaching alerts |
| Refresh your browser and rejoin | Control the meeting |

---

## When the Meeting Ends

The page will display **"This meeting has ended"** and the panels will dim automatically. The link will no longer be accessible after the meeting stops.

---

## Troubleshooting

**The link won't open**
→ Check that Tailscale shows "Connected" on your device
→ Ask your host to confirm their Tailscale is also showing "Connected"
→ Try opening the link in a different browser

**The page loads but shows no data**
→ The meeting may not have started yet — wait for your host to confirm
→ Refresh the page and re-enter your name

**I can't install Tailscale on my work laptop**
→ Try on your personal phone or personal laptop instead
→ Tailscale is available on iOS and Android as well

---

## Privacy Note

MeetingMind processes all audio and meeting data locally on the host's machine. You are only receiving display output (fact-check cards and key facts) — no audio, no transcript, and no personal data is transmitted to you or stored in the cloud.
